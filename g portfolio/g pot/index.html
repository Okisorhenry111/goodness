About Me

Hi, I'm Goodness — yes, that's really my name, and I'd like to think it fits me well.

I'm currently a student, navigating through books, assignments, and figuring out life one day at a time. But beyond the textbooks and lectures, there's so much more to who I am.

I'm someone who cherishes the people in my life deeply. I believe that the people we meet are not by accident — they are part of our story. And my story? It's filled with some of the most beautiful souls I've ever known.

My Journey

The Beginning

Life started as it does for everyone — full of wonder, curiosity, and the comfort of family. Growing up, I learned to appreciate the little things: laughter at dinner, warm hugs, and the feeling of being loved.

Becoming a Student

School brought its own adventures. The early mornings, the tough exams, the friends made in corridors and classrooms. Being a student isn't just about studying — it's about growing, making mistakes, and finding yourself along the way.

Meeting My People

Along the way, I was blessed to meet people who would change my life forever. Friends who became family. People who saw me at my worst and stayed anyway. People who made the hard days softer and the good days unforgettable.

Then Came Henry

And then, like a plot twist I never saw coming, Henry walked into my life. He didn't just enter — he made himself at home in my heart. And everything changed. Suddenly, my world had this whole new color, this whole new warmth. He made it special in ways I didn't know I needed.

Right Now

Today, I'm here — living, learning, loving, and being grateful for every single person who has been part of my journey. I may not have it all figured out, but I know one thing for sure: I am surrounded by love. And that's everything.

My Henry

"You came into my life and made it special."

Henry. My one and only boyfriend. The boy who walked into my world and turned the lights on in places I didn't even know were dark.

He is special. Not the kind of special you read about in books — the real kind. The kind that makes you smile at your phone for no reason. The kind that gives you butterflies even when you're just thinking about them.

I love annoying him. It's honestly one of my favourite things. The eye rolls, the fake sighs, the "babe, stop" — it's our language. Even though we don't talk as much as I'd want to sometimes, every word, every moment, every little thing between us is real and it matters.

He made my life special just by being in it. And I love him so much for that. For everything. For him.

♥

To Henry — my heart is yours. Always.
